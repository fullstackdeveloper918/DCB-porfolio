About DCB

David Campbell Building Pty Ltd (DCB) is a multi-award winning builder, specialising in the construction of architecturally designed residential buildings, extensions, renovations and interiors. DCB was established in 1999 by owner and Director, David Campbell. David has always maintained a principled approach to business “trust and tradition” and has invested in employing the best team of skilled tradesmen within DCB. We cover every aspect of the building process, delivering a consistent and cohesive result.

David Campbell

DIRECTOR

David Campbell heralds from entrepreneurial blood – his 3rd Great Grandfather, Robert Campbell, was Australia’s original merchant in Campbell & Co.

David, a carpenter by trade, is passionate about creating buildings of exceptional quality and durability. He has pioneered his very own site management software which has been downloaded by tens of thousands of users from all around the world.

David is an active member and former President of the Manly division of Master Builders Association (MBA) and a member of the Housing Industry of Australia (HIA).

He is also a member of the prestigious National Association of Home Builders (NAHB) and their Craftsmen Builder 20 Club in the U.S.
